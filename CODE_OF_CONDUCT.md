# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to a respectful, safe, and inclusive community for all contributors.
New contributors are welcome.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical issues, not people
- Accept feedback professionally
- Help keep discussions welcoming

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- Sharing private information without permission
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to `support@packmovego.com`.
Please do not post personal or sensitive information in public issues or pull requests.
